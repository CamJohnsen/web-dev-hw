Pico De Gallo So Good That It'll Make You Slap Your Momma!

Have you ever gone to a Mexican restaurant and been severely disappointed by their in-house made pico de gallo that accompanies the chips? Well fear no more, this fresh pico recipe full of fresh tomatoes, onions, peppers and herbs will have you salivating and clamoring for its addictive flavor!
